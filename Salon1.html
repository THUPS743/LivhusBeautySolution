About us

Livhus Beauty Solution began as a childhood dream nurtured by Livhuwani Nematserani in the heart of Maraxwe Village. As a young girl, Livhuwani often assisted the late Mrs. Grace Mudau while she braided client's hair. It was during these formative years that her passion for hair and beauty blossomed.

In 2000, Livhuwani took a significant step toward her dream by enrolling at the Mark Bernad School of Beauty in Johannesburg. She successfully completed a hair braiding course and earned her diploma, returning to Venda to start her professional journey. Her first salon space was a humble backroom at her father's butchery, where she began building a loyal clientele.

Driven by a desire to grow and refine her skills, Livhuwani returned to Johannesburg two years later to pursue further certification. This journey led her to open her first full salon in Atteridgeville, Pretoria, in 2007. The salon served the community successfully for over a decade.

However, in 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic brought unexpected challenges. Due to lockdown restrictions, Livhuwani had to close the salon and continue her work from home. This transition marked the beginning of a new chapter as Livhus Beauty Solution relocated to its current home in Lotus Gardens Central Park, where we continue to thrive and serve our valued customers with dedication and passion.
